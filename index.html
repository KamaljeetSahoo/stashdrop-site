Questions

Good to know

Do I need internet or an account?

No. Stashdrop works entirely on your local network or your iPhone's hotspot. No servers, no sign-ups, no subscription.

What if a transfer gets interrupted?

Nothing bad. A file only enters your vault after it fully arrives and its hash matches. Partials are discarded; retries never duplicate.

I use iCloud Photos — can I still use this?

Yes, as a great bulk-transfer tool. Note that with iCloud Photos on, deleting from the phone also removes from iCloud — which is why freeing space is strictly optional and clearly labeled.

Where do files end up on my Mac?

A plain Stashdrop folder in your home directory, organized by date. Ordinary files — browse them in Finder, drag them to any drive.
